Hi, I’m Dontai. I was born and raised in Chicago and am currently majoring in game design. By the end of this semester, I’m aiming for a GPA of 3.0 or higher, and when I graduate from IIT College, my goal is to finish with a 4.0 GPA and secure a job right out of school.


In my free time, I watch a lot of movies, including The Great Gatsby, Marvel films, and horror movies like Incantation on Netflix. I’m also a fan of the Lost Footage horror franchise. Besides movies, I enjoy working on and listening to and writting music(lyrics). Though I don’t play games as often because I’m busy studying, music is a big part of my life.


This past summer, I worked a family landscaping job in Colorado, which I really enjoyed. I learned how to dig trenches, lay grass, and handle various aspects of landscaping. The team I worked with was great, and I made some good friends and memories. Plus, the pay was a nice bonus!


Back in middle school, I had a Chihuahua. I loved him, though I was a reckless kid at the time. Sadly, he passed away later on, not from that incident but due to a seizure caused by old age and health issues.


As for my dreams, I want to make good music and earn money from it. I’m not after fame, just financial success. My biggest aspiration is to become an entrepreneur. I’m interested in IT, graphic design, music, and even starting a car rental business. My father’s an entrepreneur too—he started a trucking business in Colorado that’s doing really well. I’ve had the chance to go on a few trips with him, and my favorite was driving through Aspen. The mountains were stunning, and it felt like something out of a Disney movie.
